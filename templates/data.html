  • The file must be in CSV (Comma Separated Values) format.
  • Required columns: Date, Amount. Make sure they are spelled and alphabetized correctly.
  • Date: All dates must be valid and in YYYY-MM-DD format.
  • Amount: All values must be numeric, positive or negative, representing the amount. Unrealistically large amounts will cause an error.
  • Description: Not necessary, but contains a brief text description of the transaction that doesn't take up more than one cell.

Follow these steps to generate a CSV link:

  • For Google Sheets:
    1. Open your sheet in Google Sheets.
    2. Click File > Share > Publish to the web.
    3. Select the sheet to publish, choose .csv format, and copy the generated link.
  • For Excel:
    1. Save your file as .csv (File > Save As > CSV).
    2. Upload it to a file-sharing service (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive).
    3. Generate a shareable link and ensure it points to the CSV file.
  • For Any Other Sheet Editor:
    • If you don't know how to generate a csv link from your file, you can use google and youtube to help you.
